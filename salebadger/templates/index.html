This is Salebadger

Salebadger is a location-aware groupon that lets you find nearby discount deals
and share it with friends easily.

Features

Browse and
Pull it

Good deals are hard to come by. The application will automatically pinpoint your current location and presents you the nearest and most popular deals in town. Pull it as fast as you can!

Use it or
Give it away

Use it for yourself or give it away. Simply present the coupon to the retail store with your mobile phone, or you can give it away to your friend by sending through the application.
